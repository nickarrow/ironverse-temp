The Apollo Center is the main base for The [[Polestar Institute]].


The station is run by the Administration Department, the head of the station is Director [[Althea Taylan]].

The station is a research station and all residents are Polestar staff so there is no organized crime on the station, but there's a shady guy by the port who can 'get you anything you want' for the right price.

There is a bar and a diner where people can meet up or hang out.  There are no real facilities for visitors - the station doesn't produce anything for trade so not a lot of people come through.  It's not hidden, but it's pretty obscure - not a lot of people have heard of it.
