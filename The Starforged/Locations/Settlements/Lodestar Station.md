Built out of an enormous wrecked ship.

They buy ores from miners at the local asteroid field, process the ores, and sell the finished resources to traders.

There is a water shortage on the station - a pay dispute with their regular suppliers has left them with only recycled water and water rationing until they can either resolve the dispute or find a new supplier.

The station is headed by a manager.

There's a gang operating out of the docks that deals in illicit goods, the guards look the other way as long as there's no violence.

The wrecked ship's weaponry seems to be operational and can be used to defend the station.  Internally, there are guards but trouble is rare and unexpected outside of bar brawls between miners.

There are a couple of bars for visiting traders, as well as a market area for deals to take place.
