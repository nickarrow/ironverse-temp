# Hope

Hope is a spendable resource that fuels class features, domain abilities, and other special effects. It represents moments of determination, luck, and heroic resolve.

## Abilities

* [x] **Earn Hope:** On any action roll, if your action die shows 4, 5, or 6, gain +1 Hope (max 6). On a progress roll, if you have 6+ boxes filled, gain +1 Hope. On a matched strong hit, gain +2 Hope instead.

## Controls

* hope (condition meter, max: 6)
