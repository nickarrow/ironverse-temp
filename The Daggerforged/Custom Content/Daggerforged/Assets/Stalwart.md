# Stalwart

Play the Stalwart if you want to take heavy blows and keep fighting.

## Abilities

### Foundation

* [x] **Unwavering:** Reduce all harm you suffer by 1 (minimum 1).
* [x] **Iron Will:** When you suffer physical harm, you may **suffer -1 supply** to reduce the harm by 1 additional point.

### Specialization

* [ ] **Unrelenting:** Increase your harm reduction from Unwavering to 2 (minimum 1).
* [ ] **Partners-in-Arms:** When an ally within close quarters suffers harm, you may **suffer -1 supply** to reduce their harm by 1.

### Mastery

* [ ] **Undaunted:** Increase your harm reduction from Unwavering to 3 (minimum 1).
* [ ] **Loyal Protector:** When an ally you can see has 2 or fewer health and would suffer harm, you may **suffer -1 Spirit** to rush to their side and suffer the harm instead.
